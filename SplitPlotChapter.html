16.2 Mean Square R(T)

R(T) is read as “reader nested within treatment” (Hillis 2014).

\[\begin{equation} \text{MS[R(T)]}=\frac{1}{I(J-1)}\sum_{i=1}^{I}\sum_{j=1}^{J}\left ( \theta_{ij} - \theta_{i\bullet} \right )^{2} \tag{16.1} \end{equation}\]

\[\begin{equation} \text{MS[R(T)]}=\frac{1}{I}\sum_{i=1}^{I}\frac{1}{J_i-1}\sum_{j=1}^{J}\left ( \theta_{ij} - \theta_{i\bullet} \right )^{2} \tag{16.2} \end{equation}\]
